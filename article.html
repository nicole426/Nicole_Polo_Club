Polo Club’s Clubhouse Transformation: A Multimillion-Dollar Investment in Your Lifestyle—and Your Home’s Value

By Samantha Gornstein | The Edmund Bogen Team at Douglas Elliman Real Estate
 

If you’ve been thinking about selling your home at Polo Club, here’s something you need to know: the club is in the middle of a major clubhouse renovation that is set to redefine the member experience—and it’s happening right now.

As someone who sells real estate in South Florida’s premier club communities every day, I can tell you from experience: when a club invests at this level, it sends a powerful signal to buyers. It says this community is thriving, it’s forward-looking, and the best is yet to come.

Let’s break it down.

What’s Changing at the Clubhouse?

Polo Club’s Board of Governors has rolled out an ambitious renovation timeline that touches nearly every dining and social venue in the clubhouse. From reimagined restaurants to new family-friendly amenities, the transformation is comprehensive. Here’s what members—and prospective buyers—can look forward to:

Venue Est. Completion Details
Polo Prime End of April Formerly American Bistro—first to reopen with a fresh new identity
Winner’s Circle End of May A beloved member favorite, returning with an updated look and feel
Harvest Grove End of July Formerly Steeplechase—reimagined with a new concept and name
Barefoot Cafe November Renovations underway for the club’s popular casual dining spot
Laurels (2nd Floor) December Member events and private party venue—work begins once first-floor dining reopens
Splash Pad & Youth Area End of November New family-friendly amenities for the next generation of members

Key Takeaway

All timing estimates are subject to change. The Board of Governors has committed to communicating updates as new developments occur. Members can submit questions via boardfeedback@poloclub.net or through the Polo App.

What This Means If You’re Thinking About Selling

Buyers in luxury club communities aren’t just purchasing a home—they’re buying into a lifestyle. When a club invests heavily in renovating its dining, social, and family amenities, it tells prospective buyers that the community is well-managed, financially healthy, and committed to long-term excellence. That kind of confidence is exactly what drives premium offers.

With six major venues being transformed between now and December, Polo Club is positioned to showcase a completely refreshed clubhouse experience—right as seasonal buyers begin their search. Sellers who time their listings strategically stand to benefit from this momentum.

The math is compelling: a clubhouse renovation of this scale enhances the entire community’s appeal, and that appeal translates directly into buyer demand and stronger pricing for sellers.

Thinking About Your Next Move?

If you’ve been considering selling your home at Polo Club, this renovation cycle creates a unique window of opportunity. Buyers are paying attention to communities that invest in their future—and Polo Club is doing exactly that.
